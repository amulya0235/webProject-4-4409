Lassi Shop is a chain of quick service juice shops serving varieties of Indian ethnic beverages including lassi and faluda and varieties of fruit juices, smoothies, mocktails and cold coffee.

If variety is the spice of life, our menu is an assortment for those with the sweet tooth. Along with delicious flavours of Lassi, we serve Falooadas, mocktails, juices, smoothies, milkshakes, and yeah, we serve it with lots of love! We use natural colouring agents extracted from carrots, beetroots etc and that goes on to say we use quality to win the trust of our customers.
